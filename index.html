About Me

I am Sarah, a dedicated fashion stylist and styling assistant since 2019. Specializing in collaborative projects, I bring creativity and precision to crafting compelling visual narratives through clothing and accessories. From wardrobe coordination to conceptualizing and executing styling concepts, my work emphasizes meticulous attention to detail, trend awareness, and a focus on enhancing the client's vision.
